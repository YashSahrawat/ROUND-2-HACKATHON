DUBARE ELEPHANT CAMP

Introduction

Dubare elephant camp, Coorg is one of the major elephant camps among the others in Karnataka, which deals with about 150 elephants. Dubare elephant camp, on the banks of River Kaveri, is in the Kodagu/Coorg district of Karnataka. Once a place to train the elephants for Dussehra in Mysore, now has a new and enhanced calling for the world to explore.

About the camp

In Dubare elephant camp, you could get close to these gigantic creatures and interact with them, apart from indulging in other adventure activities. Dubare elephant camp is the property of the Karnataka government-owned Jungle Lodges and resorts. It is one of the important places to visit while holidaying in Coorg.

Activities in Dubare Elephant Camp

In Dubare elephant camp, tourists not only get to watch the elephants but get to interact with them as well. A trained naturalist explains the elephant’s history, biology and ecology. Tourists can also engage in bathing the elephants and feeding them in order to get an intimate experience with these animals in the presence of well-trained mahouts. Visitors can caress and closely interact with the elephants with the assigned mahouts or simply watch their activities from afar. Tourists can bathe the elephants, which gather around 9 AM by the river banks of Kaveri for their usual ritual of bathing for about 45 minutes.

Elephant Safari

An elephant safari into the jungle is always an interesting activity for tourists and wildlife enthusiasts. Dubare elephant camp organises elephant safaris for its visitors on well-trained elephants, escorted by specialists. Elephant safari timings in the Dubare elephant camp are from 10 AM to 12 PM and from 4 PM to 5 PM, respectively. The elephant ride lasts for about 45 to 60 minutes in and around the jungle.

Jeep Safari Jeep safari into the Dubare forest can be a thrilling experience for the guests who accommodate themselves in the Jungle Lodges and Resorts. Guests can spot animals like Sambar, spotted deer, gaur, wild elephants, sloth bears, tigers, bison, crocodiles, etc which infest the forest. The jeep safari is an exciting one for bird lovers as well, for the Dubare forest is home to a variety of bird species like Parakeets, Barbets, Hornbills, Peacocks, Kingfishers, Partridges and others. Camping Dubare elephant camp has something for all. It doesn’t disappoint the nature lovers as well. Apart from engaging with elephants and taking part in wildlife activities, camping is also an option to spend a worthwhile weekend. The picnic spots and camping areas are safely fenced with electric fences to dissuade the intrusion of wild animals.
